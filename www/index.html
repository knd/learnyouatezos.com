Learn You a Tezos for Much Prosperity

Learn You a Tezos for Much Prosperity is a free e-book that will help you get started with the Tezos blockchain.

This book will be helpful for founders / CTOs deciding on adoption, aspiring bakers, smart contract authors and people just wanting to run a node for fun.

The tutorials within assume little to no prior knowledge of blockchain technologies, server administration or software development.

They are also in-depth enough that they should be useful for people who are experienced with any of the above.
